Violet the Smash Player

Hailing from Northeast Ohio, Rosa "Violet" Kilmer is a Joker and Sonic comain who's been in the offline Smash Ultimate scene since 2021. She can be regularly seen at Kent Combo tournaments. She's also a part of the Spinda's Cafe online Smash crew where she's been a member for the entire lifetime of the crew. She has an offline win rate of 30% for sets and a 35% win rate for individual games. While having no notable placements in majors or regionals, she still likes to attend tournaments each week in the hopes of having fun and getting better and growing as a player.

Rosa at Home

Outside of Smash, Rosa is currently a senior Computer Science student at Kent State University. She's been studying to become a game programmer or even a software engineer. At 22 years old, she's worked at the Kent State University Service and Repair Center as a computer repair technician since 2020. Her interest in technology and computers motivates her to keep learning and growing as a programmer. In her free time, she enjoys playing other video games, especially Sonic games and Persona. Currently, she is working on finishing Persona 3 Reload, the Emblems in Sonic Adventure DX, and Final Fantasy VII Rebirth.
